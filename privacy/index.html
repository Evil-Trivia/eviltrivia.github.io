Privacy Policy

Evil Trivia (eviltrivia.com)

Last updated: April 26, 2026

This policy describes how Evil Trivia (“we,” “us”) handles information when you use our website or when you participate in our SMS-based trivia idea submission program.

Information we collect

  • Phone number. If you text our program number, your mobile telephone number is received by our messaging provider as part of delivering the message.
  • Message content. The text you send (for example, trivia ideas or related notes) is collected so we can review and use it for show-related purposes.
  • Technical data. Like most websites, our hosting and analytics tools may collect standard technical information (such as browser type, general location derived from IP, and pages viewed) when you visit eviltrivia.com.

How we use information

We use the information above to operate the site, receive and review trivia submissions, maintain records (including in spreadsheets or similar internal tools), and communicate with you only as needed to respond to your messages or operate the program.

Sharing and marketing

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share phone numbers or message content with third parties for their own marketing. We may use service providers (for example, SMS and hosting vendors) solely to deliver and operate the service, subject to their role as processors on our behalf.

Retention

We retain submissions and related logs only as long as needed for production, troubleshooting, and legal compliance, then delete or anonymize them where reasonable.

Your choices

You may stop SMS messages from our program by following the opt-out instructions in our Terms and Conditions for SMS.

Contact

Questions about this policy may be directed through the contact options published on eviltrivia.com.

This is a general notice for a small project site and is not tailored legal advice. Update it if your data practices change.
